About Me

my name is mary & i am a front-end developer

Hello everyone! I find passion in problem solving and in seeing new designs come to life. I'm a biology and chemistry graduate who realized after many years in the lab that coding is my true passion. Recently I enrolled in Launch School - a mastery based program on the track to becoming a Software Engineer. I have always been someone who is constantly learning, so I jumped at the opportunity to learn development. I am a person who is incredibly self-disciplined and always looks for a solution when things get difficult.

A few fun facts about myself: I live in Chicago. I love craft beers, and when I'm not working on a website or new app you can find me outdoors, playing cozy games, or reading my latest book club pick.
